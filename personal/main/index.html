Mechatronics

Qualified in Mechatronics and Electrotechnics. I am constantly learning about new technologies and improving my skills to develop my personal projects in Automation, Robotics and IoT. My newest project is an autonomous robot produced by a 3D printer which I call miniAGV. This project have been developed by me in those fields 3D Desgin, Electronics, the Control System and the Software. This robot is an inspiration from my final project in my degree, when I developed a prototype of and AGV (Automated Guided Vehicle), but a robust one made of aluminium and in industrial dimensions.

Triathlon

As an amatuer triathlete, I have been running several Half Marathons and short Triathlon distances in the last years. Since then I finished 3 Ironman 70.3 and a few more competitions with the same distance. In 2018 I ran my first Ultra Marathon in a race called Desafio 50K.
